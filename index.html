About Us

Welcome to Kelly's!

Looking for the best Hot Wings in Northeast PA?

Well, you have come to the right place! In addition to our famous hot wings we have a full menu with something for everyone. We are a family oriented restaurant in Scranton, PA we have a reputation for great food, great people and the best hot wings in Northeast Pennsylvania.

Kelly's Wednesday Wing Night has become a very popular spot for Hot Wings and ice cold beer served in a friendly family atmosphere. Our Hot Wings have been honored with many awards in local wing contests and media surveys throughout Northeastern Pennsylvania. In addition to our wing night we have $2.00 domestic Pint night every Monday. Tuesday's are Tini' Tuesday at Kelly's. We have Martini specials for $4.00. Every lent we offer fish specials.

With more than 25 years in business we have established ourselves as the place to go for Hot Wings in Scranton, PA! Kelly's pub & Eatery was established on July 26, 1990. This family business is located in South Scranton at 1802 Cedar Avenue - Scranton, PA 18505. Call us at 570-346-9758 for take out orders.
